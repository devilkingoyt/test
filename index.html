About Us

Crescent Bahria Cadet College (CBCC) is a prestigious institution that offers a wide range of programs and activities for cadets. Established in 1987, CBCC has grown to become one of the leading cadet colleges in Pakistan, providing quality education and opportunities for students to grow and develop.

CBCC has a strong commitment to fostering a diverse and inclusive learning environment, promoting critical thinking and problem-solving skills, and encouraging creativity and innovation. Through its extensive facilities, scholarships, and partnerships, CBCC aims to provide a safe and supportive learning environment for students.

CBCC is committed to promoting excellence and providing opportunities for students to excel in their academic and personal lives. We strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment for all students, regardless of their background or abilities.
